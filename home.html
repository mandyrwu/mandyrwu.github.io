SWE Hacks '22

Currently, I am working on the advertisement and branding assets for UW SWE's 2022 Hackathon. This includes making an event logo, banners, social media posts, and more. All illustrations were made via Sketchbook and designs are made via Figma. Some creations are in collaboration w/ the SWE Hacks '22 PR team.

Birdie

Birdie is a personal blog that my friend and I started over the summer. The site was made on SquareSpace and all the contents were c-created by my friend and I. In this project, I learned about site design, search engine optimization, and also managing social media for branding purposes.

Chicken Tracker

Chicken Tracker is a prototyping assignment that was created in my HCDE210 class during the Au21 quarter. The assignment involved lofi-prototyping with sketches, then wireframing the flow of the different pages, and lastly hi-fi prototyping in Figma. I learned to use Figma during this project, and also explored different UI elements.

BUZZStop98040
